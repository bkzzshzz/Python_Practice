Crow

Dark the color cawing all the way
Having fear travelling around a place
Seeking no help from any human at all
A crow I see at my roof; standing tall.

I see it wet because of the rain
When I go close, it chooses another lane
My cane is too slow for him I know
But he still fears me the dark colored crow

Many years have passed but he's still the same
Even same is his the famous black name
But my life has changed me this day
So all I have is the crows to play.

Happy Birthday

Have you ever wondered, if
All the stars shone at once?
People would go blinded at once
People would go crazy at once, but
You would still shine the most.

Before this happiness could vanish
I will always remember you
Reading my old poems
Turning the old pages, again.
Hope that you would still remember me, like
Days of summer for bumblebees
And nights of wonder for all the seas, and
Yes, the butterflies that you give.

A boy still thinks about you, though
Many months have gone so silent.
I have no words to keep but
To think of what we could be
All the same, or a little different.

God?

If someone could explain to me
Why I was born this way
If someone could tell me
Why we count life in days
If anyone could handle me
From all those misery haze
Then I would make my life
The life that everyone would gaze
In the limitless sky
With a hope to find himself
And just to give it a try
If really the one that made him
Exists in the palace of clouds
At which we frequently shout
To our supreme belief I guess
Has provided us nothing but less
Lesser than an ant, a sloth
The peace of mind and heart on earth
The one that cannot be explained
In the language of physics
In the language of literature
Even we know the failure
Of mathematics
To know what's there in and beyond
The infinity, the divinity, the trinity
There's no answer to everything
If someone could explain me
What is god?

I miss you

the ring goes on and on, you dont receive
but i know the network would never deceive
either you listen to the voice mails i sent
or you see my missed calls, which are unseen
you pretend.

you take your pictures, you do edit them sometimes
but you dont need no edits, cuz your face got the perfect rhymes
a poet would never unnotice the glamour pass by
his best poems would just break away, cuz they were all
just lies

the melody of your ringtone is i know something you love
and you know i hate the calling sound but i can't make it stop
i wonder if you see my name on the top of your contact lists
or at the bottom or nowhere; like a dew lost into
the mist

I miss you....dear

Come let's swing by the old songs that we listened to
Come let's rejoice the old memories that we can remember
Come let's walk on the pavements that are still the same
Come let's build up the promises that we made for ever.

Come let's sing with our ugly voices once again
Come let's make jokes and make people mad again
Come let's fool around our small town once again
Come let's talk and keep on talking time and again.

Come let's simplify the life problems that we go through
Come let's hang on the happiness that we loved back then
Come let's make magic happen when its pitch dark
Come let's rewire our brains and go insanely insane.

Come let's throw pebbles on the pond and river nearby
Come let's count the stars and forget the counting
Come let's watch the green leaves turn brown and green again
Come let's forget about the dead and think about living.

But i know
The things written above
Would never come true.
I miss you dear
Where are you?

आजकल

दिनभरी
चिन्ता गर्दै,
धिक्कार मान्दै,
समयको गतिलाई सराप्दै,
आजकल दिन भर्दो रहेछ।
बितेका सुनौला पल सम्झदै
खाएका कसमहरु केलाउदै
भिजेका सपनाहरु सुकाउदै
आजकल दिन भर्दो रहेछ।
माया लाएका कुराहरु गनगनाउदै
आफ्नै सायालाई आँफैले पछ्याउदै
सही र गलतलाई छुत्याउदै
आजकल दिन भर्दो रहेछ।
आइन्स्ताइनलाई सही तुल्याउदै
पिक्कासोको जय गुणगान गाउदै
नारायण गोपाललाई सम्झाउदै
आजकल दिन भर्दो रहेछ।
पुराना पुस्तकहरु थन्काउदै
नयाँ लुगाहरु लगाउदै
आँफैलाई आफु आत्मसमर्पन गर्दै
आजकल दिन भर्दो रहेछ।
